PHYLOSOPHY

MANUFACTURE D’ESSAI has its own definite, recognisable style born of its skill in merging its Italian background, based on impeccable craftsmanship, with modern, sophisticated taste. A timeless approach rooted in precise, clean-cut, but not minimalist refinement; up-to-the-minute design and constant experimentation with form, combined with the constant of experience. It stands for modern stylishness always in tune with the trend of the day. Carefully selected high-quality materials and refined, but discreet, finishing contribute to the uniqueness of the Manufacture D’Essai collections, in which craftsmanship and technology are perfectly balanced. Style takes precedence over fashion, substance over superficiality, the awareness of individual personality over belonging to a “type.” It places a new dimension on leisure time and versatility, never forgoing glamour and style.
